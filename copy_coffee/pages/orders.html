Logistics & Roasting Schedule

When is my coffee roasted?

We roast every Tuesday and Thursday morning. Orders received before midnight are roasted in the next immediate batch to ensure maximum freshness.

How long is shipping?

We ship nationwide via DHL/FedEx Express. Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. You will receive tracking logs as soon as the package is collected.

Are packaging bags recyclable?

Yes, our bags are made of 100% compostable Kraft paper with plant-based high-barrier lining. Simply remove the tin-tie before composting.
